A commerce-focused release that turns 3DPrintForge into a small print shop. Orders now show real profitability, a customer-facing storefront sells your catalog, orders flow straight to the print queue, and the buy side gains shipment tracking, a cross-supplier shopping list and automatic reordering — all built on the filament costs the dashboard already tracks.

<!-- truncate -->

## Order profitability & margins

- The CRM dashboard has a **Profitability** section: revenue vs cost of goods (COGS), **margin and margin %**, a margin-by-month chart and your most profitable products
- Every order detail carries its own profitability card — revenue, COGS and margin — derived from the actual per-item filament, labour, electricity and wear cost
- New read-only endpoints: `GET /api/crm/orders/:id/margin`, `/api/crm/margins/products`, `/api/crm/margins/summary`

## Storefront & product catalog

- A new **Products** catalog (Business → Products): sellable products with a sale price and a per-unit cost breakdown, and a **live margin** preview as you edit
- A customer-facing shop at **`/shop`** — a self-contained page where visitors browse active products and place an order. Orders land in your CRM as **pending**, ready to price and produce
- The public API exposes only public-safe fields (no cost, margin or file data), validates every order before writing, enforces stock server-side, and is rate-limited

## Order to print queue

- Add catalog products to a CRM order, then **dispatch its printable lines to the print queue** in one click
- Stock is deducted on add; each dispatched line shows an **"in queue"** badge so you can follow fulfilment from order to print

## Purchase order shipment tracking

- Purchase orders gain a **Shipped** stage between placed and received — record the **carrier and tracking number**
- One-click **Track** link for common carriers (PostNord, Posten, Bring, DHL, UPS, FedEx, GLS, USPS)

## Smarter buying

- A **Shopping list** on the Reorder card turns low stock into a per-shop basket — the cheapest supplier per material with **Buy** links, suggested quantity, line cost and per-supplier subtotals
- **Auto-reorder**: switch it on to have the server periodically draft purchase orders (or just notify you) when a material drops below its minimum target — deduped so scheduler runs never stack duplicate drafts

## Under the hood

- Migrations 161–163; roughly 90 new automated tests across the profitability, storefront, fulfilment and buy-side changes
- Security review of the unauthenticated storefront path (auth-gate hardening, stock clamp, a dedicated per-IP order limiter, input caps)
